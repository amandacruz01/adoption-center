Welcome! We're glad you're here

Hello! My name is Amanda, I am the owner of this organization. This organization is used for animals to be adopted into a loving family. I founded this organization in 2018 because I think that all animals deserve a happy home. Most of our animals were found on the street, hurt, hungry or sad. We've tried to find their owners, but after a few months we think that they deserve someone to love them.
